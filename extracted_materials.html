- remote-first businesses

Every recurring service should have an internal owner.

Move important or high-risk services onto dedicated virtual cards.

- critical tools

Once renewals are structured, review them on a schedule.

- what is still active

Google’s helpful-content guidance emphasizes transparency about who is behind a site and whether information is presented in a way that makes users want to trust it. [developers...google.com], [developers...google.com]
  • For payment-adjacent trust/disclosure pages, FinancialService can be appropriate where the page is about the service offering, fees, payment methods, or supported workflows. Schema.org explicitly defines FinancialService for financial services businesses and supports properties like accepted payment methods, currencies accepted, area served, and fees/commissions.
